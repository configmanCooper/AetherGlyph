# Solo Tutorial Campaign

The tutorial is fully offline and uses the production gesture recognizer and deterministic simulation.

## Teaching rules

- Full template, then dotted template, then start marker, then no guide.
- The player receives a short reason for every rejection.
- A lesson cannot require a spell not previously taught.
- Optional mastery medals never gate ranked play.
- The bot uses legal resources and spells.
- Core ranked unlock occurs after Lesson 12.

## Campaign

### Prologue - The Ink Awakens

Teach:

- hold to draw,
- release to submit,
- Break Trace,
- trace confidence,
- first-person opponent view.

Spell: Ember Bolt.

Pass: land three Ember Bolts, the last without a template.

### Lesson 1 - Aether Breath

Teach:

- Aether pool and regeneration,
- why rapid spam creates vulnerability,
- minimum cast recovery.

Spells: Ember Bolt, Brace.

Pass: damage the target, preserve 20 Aether, then Brace an incoming bolt.

### Lesson 2 - The Moving Line

Teach:

- left-thumb movement arc,
- Sidestep charges,
- projectile travel,
- drawing while repositioning.

Spell: Stone Shard.

Pass: evade two bolts and land a Stone Shard through the opening.

### Lesson 3 - Wards and Angles

Teach:

- directional Ward,
- attacks around a ward,
- defense is valuable only when timed.

Spells: Ward, Flame Wave.

Pass: block 20 damage, then angle outside the instructor's Ward and land Flame Wave.

### Lesson 4 - Clean Hands

Teach:

- status icons,
- Burning and Chilled,
- Dispel timing.

Spells: Frost Lance, Dispel.

Pass: apply Chilled, cleanse Burning, and avoid wasting Dispel on direct damage.

### Lesson 5 - Focus Under Fire

Teach:

- Sigil Charges,
- visible charge count,
- interruptible Focus,
- charge decay.

Spells: Focus, Concussive Blast.

Pass: gain one charge behind a safe opening and interrupt the instructor's Focus.

### Lesson 6 - Mark and Release

Teach:

- setup versus payoff,
- Amplify Mark,
- burst cap and opponent action window.

Spells: Amplify, Arcane Missile.

Pass: apply Mark and consume it with Arcane Missile after the instructor's defense expires.

### Lesson 7 - Rain Conducts

Teach:

- shared zones,
- Soaked,
- lightning reaction,
- opponent can exploit your weather.

Spells: Rain Glyph, Spark Dart.

Pass: shock the instructor in Wet ground, then avoid the instructor's return lightning.

### Lesson 8 - Fire Changes the Ground

Teach:

- Oil Script,
- ignition,
- Rain counter,
- zone cap.

Spells: Oil Script, Ember Bolt, Rain Glyph.

Pass: ignite one Oil zone and douse another before the instructor ignites it.

### Lesson 9 - The Cold Lock

Teach:

- Chilled prerequisite,
- Frost Bind,
- Tenacity,
- hard-control cannot chain.

Spells: Frost Lance, Frost Bind, Blink.

Pass: execute one legal freeze and Blink away from the instructor's attempted follow-up.

### Lesson 10 - Wind Answers

Teach:

- Gust deflection,
- Fog clearing,
- Oil movement,
- no defense answers everything.

Spells: Gust Wall, Fog Cloud.

Pass: deflect a light projectile, clear Fog, and avoid a heavy Stone Shard that Gust cannot stop.

### Lesson 11 - Cover and Ruin

Teach:

- Stone Wall,
- line of sight,
- Focus behind cover,
- Quake and Fireball counters.

Spells: Stone Wall, Quake.

Pass: use cover safely once, then destroy the instructor's wall during Focus.

### Lesson 12 - First Formal Duel

Teach:

- loadout builder,
- point budget,
- round timer,
- Arcane Pressure,
- post-match counter report.

Pass: win one full legal round against the Adept instructor.

Reward: ranked eligibility and all public competitive spells.

## Advanced academy

### Lesson 13 - Resource War

Spells: Aether Surge, Aether Leech, Weaken, Haste, Grounding Mantle.

Goal: recognize when economy setup is safe, when pressure is more valuable, and when becoming Grounded is worth reduced movement.

### Lesson 14 - Reading Resonance

Spells: Ember Attunement plus mixed elemental loadout.

Goal: predict a repeated school, then deliberately switch school to trigger a reaction.

### Lesson 15 - Reflection Trial

Spells: Reflect, Fireball, Arcane Missile.

Goal: reflect a light/medium projectile, refuse a Fireball reflect trap, and use Barrier or movement instead.

### Lesson 16 - The Eight Schools Examination

Goal: play a best-of-three match against Magus AI using two different loadouts.

## Secret spell discoveries

Secrets are optional and become fully documented after discovery.

### Mirror Twin - "Two hands write one intention"

Clues:

- A figure-eight carving appears during Aether Surge practice in Lesson 13 and again in the advanced academy hall.
- Mastery requirement: cause the bot to defend against a canceled heavy windup twice.

Trial:

- Draw the figure eight, then tap the mirrored rune before the timer ends.

### Hourglass Field - "The sand slows all, including its keeper"

Clues:

- Finish Lesson 2 without spending both Sidestep charges.
- Inspect the stopped arena clock in the codex room.

Trial:

- Defeat a fast projectile sequence by changing shared timing rather than blocking.

### Phoenix Covenant - "A flame survives only when the enemy knows it burns"

Clues:

- Win a round at exactly 1 health.
- Complete the Oil lesson mastery medal.

Trial:

- Activate the visible covenant, bait a weak hit, and survive a later committed strike.

### Prismatic Beam - "No single color opens the spectrum"

Clues:

- Trigger five different environmental reactions.
- Complete a round using at least four schools.

Trial:

- Build two different resonance marks and complete the six-segment star line.

## Practice laboratory

- Select any unlocked spell.
- Toggle full/dotted/start-only/no template.
- Show resampled points, recognized primitive, confidence, ambiguity, duration, and common error.
- Compare only against current loadout or against the entire catalog.
- Spawn any status or environment.
- Replay successful traces locally.
- Raw traces remain on device unless explicitly attached to a bug report.
